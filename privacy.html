Privacy Policy for Spendful

Last updated: March 2026

Spendful is a calm, local-first spend awareness app. The vast majority of your data never leaves your device. This policy explains exactly what data is collected, why, and with whom it is shared — including optional AI features that do transmit data externally.

1. Data Stored on Your Device

All core app data is stored locally using your device's secure storage and is never sent to Spendful's servers. This includes:

  • Spending entries (date, amount, category, currency, note)
  • Recurring spending templates
  • Custom categories
  • Journal entries
  • App settings (theme, currency, notification preferences)
  • Subscription and trial status

You can export all of this data at any time from within the app. Exports are saved to your device and are never uploaded.

2. AI Features (Optional)

Spendful offers two optional AI-powered features. These features require an active internet connection and send data to a third-party AI provider. They are entirely optional — the app functions fully without them.

Receipt Scanning

When you use the receipt scanning feature, a photo of your receipt is converted to an image and sent to an AI service (OpenAI) to extract the amount, date, merchant name, and suggested category. The image is processed and immediately discarded — it is not stored by Spendful.

Spending Insights

When you request an AI insight, a summary of your recent spending entries (dates, amounts, categories, and any notes you have written) is sent to the AI service to generate a short personalised reflection. Previous insights are included to avoid repetition. No identifying information (name, email, device ID) is included in these requests.

What OpenAI receives: Receipt images and/or anonymised spending summaries. Requests are made directly from your device using an API key. OpenAI's data handling is governed by OpenAI's Privacy Policy. By default, OpenAI does not use API data to train models. You can review their API data usage policy at openai.com.

3. Currency Conversion (Optional)

If you change your default currency and choose to convert existing entries, Spendful contacts the Exchange Rate API (open.er-api.com) to fetch current exchange rates. Only the currency code (e.g. "USD") is sent — no personal or financial data is included.

4. In-App Purchases

Subscriptions and one-time purchases are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). Spendful does not receive or store your payment details. Purchase validation (confirming your subscription is active) is handled on-device using data returned by the platform. Apple's and Google's privacy policies govern any data they collect during the purchase flow.

5. Push Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, Spendful schedules local notifications on your device. Notifications are managed entirely on-device — no notification tokens, device identifiers, or schedules are transmitted to external servers.

6. Analytics and Tracking

Spendful contains no analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or third-party tracking of any kind. There is no telemetry, no usage metrics, and no user profiling.

8. Children's Privacy

Spendful is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
